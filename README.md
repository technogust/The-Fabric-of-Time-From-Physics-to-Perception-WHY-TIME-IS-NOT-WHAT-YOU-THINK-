# The-Fabric-of-Time-From-Physics-to-Perception-WHY-TIME-IS-NOT-WHAT-YOU-THINK-

A book by Vaibhav Sharma takes you through a mind-expanding journey through time, physics, consciousness and the hidden nature of time.

What if time is not what you think it is?

You experience time every second—but have you ever truly understood it?

In The Fabric of Time: From Physics to Perception, Vaibhav Sharma explores the true nature of time through the lens of physics, neuroscience, psychology, and philosophy—revealing how time is not a fixed reality, but a layered experience shaped by the universe and the human mind.

If you enjoy books on time, relativity, quantum physics, consciousness, and the nature of reality, this book will challenge and transform the way you think.
